"How has the growth of Flag Flag Football changed athletes and the future for the sports industry?"

Flag Football has changed many athletes and more specifically female athletes. The growing interest of flag football was so great that highschools have add a flag football team giving many girls more opportunities and a chance to play the same play of football minus the physical element.

About Flag Football

Flag football is a variant of American football minus the physical contact and substituting tackles with two or three fabric flags. Now the origin of Flag was during WWII, flag football was started for American soldiers serving in the war.Eventually, soldiers brought the idea of flag football to their homes and that's how it came to be.

In the NFL Flag website they say "In the 1990s, when the NFL launched a global health and wellness intiative called Play Football, they had one goal: to make football accessible for all children-no matter their age, size, or gender." And then that's how the NFL began their Flag program which helped grow the sport. An NFL Football Operations article says that " Flag football is one of the fastest-growing sports today, with more than 20 million participants in 100 countries and counting."

How has this sport impacted athletes?

Flag football has impacted many younger athletes with it's safer variant of tackle football. But now, girls have been given the opportunity to play at the highschool and collegiate level. NFL Commissioner Roger Goodell said that "the expansion of women's participation in flag football is not just about playing the game; it's about recognition and advancment in sports." this not only describes the growth of flag for girls but how the sports industry is almost evolving in a sense giving more athletes opportunities.

Play like a Girl, a program that helps build and recognize women of stem and sports says that "through flag football, girls learn key skills that extend beyind the game." They also say that the sport "offer girls an opportunity to explore football in a low-contact and supportive enviroment, encouraging teamwork, leadership, and confidence." The importance of the growth of womens sports shows the commitment of inclusiveness as shown when the sports "growing presence at the youth, collegiate, and professional levels is a testament to the growing interest and commitment to advancing women's participation in sports." Along side that, it was confirmed that flag football would be added to the 2028 olympics for both men and women. Showing the immense growth of Flag.
