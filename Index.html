"How has the growth of Flag Flag Football changed athletes and the future for the sports industry?"

Flag Football has changed many athletes and more specifically female athletes. The growing interest of flag football was so great that highschools have add a flag football team giving many girls more opportunities and a chance to play the same play of football minus the physical element.

About Flag Football

Flag football is a variant of American football minus the physical contact and substituting tackles with two or three fabric flags. Now the origin of Flag was during WWII, flag football was started for American soldiers serving in the war.Eventually, soldiers brought the idea of flag football to their homes and that's how it came to be.

In the NFL Flag website they say "In the 1990s, when the NFL launched a global health and wellness intiative called Play Football, they had one goal: to make football accessible for all children-no matter their age, size, or gender." And then that's how the NFL began their Flag program which helped grow the sport.
